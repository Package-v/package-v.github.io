Need Help? We're Here for You!

If you’re experiencing issues with Package-V or have any questions, our team is ready to assist you. Please don’t hesitate to reach out, and we’ll get back to you within 1 hour during our support hours.

When reaching out, kindly include:

  • A brief description of the issue you’re facing.
  • Any relevant error messages or screenshots.
  • The version of Package-V you’re using (if applicable).
  • Your GitHub repository link.

We appreciate your patience and will work to resolve your issue promptly!

Contact Support

For support, please contact us at: info@package-v.com

Support Hours

Monday to Sathurday, 9 AM to 6 PM (CT)

Please note that requests received outside of support hours may experience a delay in response, but we will attend to them as soon as possible.

Common Issues

Before contacting support, check if these common issues apply to your situation:

  • Package version not updating?

    Ensure your branch prefix follows the supported formats (e.g., fix/, feature/, !feat/).

  • Error when publishing to GitHub Registry?

    Verify that your package.json name is correctly formatted (e.g., @username/package-name or @organization/package-name).

Feature Requests and Feedback

Have a feature request or suggestion? We’d love to hear from you! Feel free to reach out with your ideas, and we’ll consider them for future updates to make Package-V even better.
