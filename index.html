WHO WE ARE?

At Package V, we are a passionate team of developers and tech enthusiasts dedicated to simplifying the software development process.

With years of experience in building and managing npm packages, we understand the challenges that developers face when it comes to version management and publishing. Our mission is to streamline these processes, enabling developers to focus on what they do best: writing code. We believe that managing versions should be straightforward and intuitive, so we created our GitHub app to help automate tedious tasks and minimize errors.

Driven by a commitment to community and collaboration, we continually seek feedback and iterate on our solutions to ensure we meet the evolving needs of developers everywhere. Join us on our journey to make version management seamless and efficient!
